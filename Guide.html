Navigate between Intro, Guide, and Atlas

On your phone, look near-right in the browser toolbar, for the tab button — the little rounded square that shows how many tabs you have open. Use it to navigate between pages. That way, they remain available for review.

Navigate the atlas

The atlas is arranged as a table. Each column is a different tree. Each row shows a different characteristic of that tree. One photo fills the screen at a time.

Swipe left / right
Move between trees. Each swipe goes to the next column. On desktop, use the arrow buttons or left/right arrow keys.
Scroll up / down
Move between characteristics. Each scroll goes to the next row. On desktop, use the mouse scroll-wheel outside the photo.

Viewing Photos Up Close

Any photo can be enlarged for a closer look.

Pinch
Two finger pinch zooms in or out. On desktop, use the scroll-wheel inside the photo.
Drag
When zoomed in, drag with one finger to pan around the photo and explore details. Just outside the photo, you can still swipe or scroll.

What the Rows Mean

Each column follows the same structure from top to bottom, so you can compare the same characteristic across different subjects by swiping left or right.
